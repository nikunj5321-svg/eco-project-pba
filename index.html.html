Turning Waste into Innovation

An advanced eco-project website by PBA Team Grandmasters, showcasing how imagination, teamwork, and sustainability can transform waste into energy, greenery, and fashion.

About the Initiative

This project demonstrates the power of reuse and recycling through three innovative eco-models built entirely from waste materials.

Eco Thinking

We learned that waste is a resource, not a problem.

Sustainability

Our models focus on renewable energy, green spaces, and recycling.

Innovation

Simple materials + imagination = meaningful solutions.

Our Projects

Three core projects representing energy, environment, and creativity.

Biogas Planter

Plastic bottles, cow dung, and food waste were used to produce biogas through anaerobic decomposition.

Vertical Gardening

Waste plastic bottles arranged vertically to grow plants in limited spaces.

Fashion from Waste

Old cloth and wrappers transformed into bags, bracelets, and accessories.

Project Workflow

Step-by-step learning journey followed by our team.

1. Planning

Brainstorming ideas and selecting eco-friendly concepts.

2. Collection

Gathering waste materials from homes and surroundings.

3. Execution

Building models using teamwork and creativity.

4. Learning

Understanding sustainability, recycling, and responsibility.
